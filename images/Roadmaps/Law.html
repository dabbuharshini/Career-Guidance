↓

5-Year Integrated (BA/BBA/B.Com LLB)

Step 5: Skill Building & Internships

Participate in debates, legal clinics, and moot courts.

↓

Step 6: Internships with Law Firms, NGOs, Legal Experts

↓

Step 7: Choose Your Specialization

Criminal Law
Corporate Law
IP Law
Cyber Law
Human Rights
Environmental Law

Step 8: Higher Education or Certifications

LLM
Judiciary Exams
Legal Research
↓

Step 9: Career Opportunities

Join law firms, judiciary, or corporate legal departments.

↓

Final Step: Continuous Learning & Legal Updates

Stay updated on legal reforms, court judgments, and skills.
